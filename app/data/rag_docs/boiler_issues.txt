Boiler Troubleshooting Guide – Professional Checklist

1. No Heating or No Hot Water
- Check boiler pressure (should be between 1 and 2 bar).
- Reset the boiler if the reset light is flashing.
- Ensure the thermostat is set to ON and above the room temperature.
- Check for error codes on the boiler display.
- Radiators not heating may indicate trapped air – bleeding may be required.

2. Boiler Keeps Turning Off
- Low water pressure.
- Frozen external condensate pipe.
- Faulty thermostat or sensor.
- Blocked or restricted flue.

3. Strange Noises (Kettling / Whistling)
- Limescale buildup on the heat exchanger.
- Low water flow.
- Air in the system.

4. When to Call an Engineer
- Smell of gas or burning.
- Boiler leaking water.
- Boiler repeatedly losing pressure.
- Carbon monoxide alarms sounding.

For emergencies, call National Gas Emergency: 0800 111 999.
