General Property Safety Instructions

1. Gas Safety
- If you smell gas, turn off the gas supply immediately.
- Open windows and avoid electrical switches.
- Leave the property and call 0800 111 999.

2. Electrical Safety
- Do not touch exposed wires.
- Lights flickering may indicate loose wiring.
- Overheating sockets should be isolated.

3. Fire Safety
- Smoke alarms must be tested every month.
- Do not block escape routes.
- If a fire breaks out, evacuate and call 999.
