General Property FAQs & Support Information

1. Heating Problems
- Thermostat should be set to HEAT mode.
- Radiators cold at top â†’ may require bleeding.
- Check timers on heating controls.

2. Internet Issues
- Restart router for 30 seconds.
- Check if service provider has an outage.
- Ensure cables are firmly connected.

3. Appliance Issues
- Washing machines not draining often have blocked filters.
- Ovens not heating may require element replacement.
- Fridge not cooling: check coils behind fridge.

4. Tenancy Questions
- Repairs classed as emergency if they affect safety, security, or essential services.
- Always document issues with photos.
- Notify landlord or agent promptly.

5. When to Contact Emergency Services
- Fire.
- Gas smell.
- Significant flooding.
- Strong burning smell.

For routine repairs, use approved property service providers.
